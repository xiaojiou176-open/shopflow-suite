Refine the Shopflow docs/front-door surface into a product-first, builder-first reading path that feels calm, confident, and non-robotic.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, desktop-first but responsive
- Theme: light, warm, editorial, route-first
- Background: Paper Canvas (#f6f1e8)
- Primary Accent: Evergreen Route (#1f6b57) for strongest actions only
- Text Primary: Ink Charcoal (#1f1c17)
- Secondary Text: Weathered Stone (#6c665d)
- No glassmorphism, no brutalism, no dashboard sludge, no packet-first intro

**Page Structure:**
1. **Hero:** Explain what Shopflow is in one calm sentence and one short proof line.
2. **Truth Boundary:** State what is real today vs what still is not claimed.
3. **First Product Path:** Give the shortest truthful reading order.
4. **Builder Side Door:** Keep builder-facing entry secondary but elegant.
